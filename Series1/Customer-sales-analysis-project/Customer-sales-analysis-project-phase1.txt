PHASE 1: CLIENT REQUEST to BUSINESS UNDERSTANDING
------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: DEFINING THE CLIENT (SIMULATION)

Client: A mid-sized retail company (online + offline sales)

Business Problem (given by client): “We have sales data but we don’t understand our customers and sales performance. We want insights to improve revenue.”


STEP 2: ASKING BUSINESS QUESTIONS
=>Customer-focused:
Who are our top customers?
Which customers generate the most revenue?
Are there repeat customers?

=>Sales-focused:
What is total sales?
What are monthly trends?
Are sales increasing or decreasing?

=>Product-focused:
Which products sell the most?
Which products underperform?


STEP 3: DEFINING BUSINESS OBJECTIVES
questions → objectives

=>Final Objectives:
Identify top customers
Analyse sales performance over time
Evaluate product performance
Provide actionable business insights

=>These will go into project documentation


STEP 4: DEFINING Key Performance Indicators(KPIs)

KPIs = metrics companies care about

=>Customer KPIs:
Total customers
Top 10 customers by revenue

=>Sales KPIs:
Total sales
Average sales
Monthly sales trend

=>Product KPIs:
Top-selling products
Least-performing products


STEP 5: DEFINING DELIVERABLES THAT CLIENT WILL GET
=>Final Output:
Dashboard
Insights report (simple summary)
Dataset
